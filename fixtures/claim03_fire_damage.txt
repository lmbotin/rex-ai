SEVERE KITCHEN FIRE - February 10, 2024 at approximately 8:00 PM. Left stove unattended for a few minutes and grease caught fire. Fire spread to kitchen cabinets and ceiling above stove. Fire department responded and extinguished the fire. Major damage to cabinets, countertop, stove, and ceiling. Smoke damage throughout the kitchen. Property located at 789 Pine Boulevard, San Jose, CA. Fire marshal report filed. Preliminary contractor estimate: $15,000-$18,000 for repairs and cleanup.
